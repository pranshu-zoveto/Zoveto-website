## Design Context

### Users
Primary audience is a mixed buying committee for Zoveto: founder/owner, operations lead, and finance lead evaluating system fit before rollout.

### Brand Personality
Operator-practical, grounded, and credible. The section should reduce risk anxiety and show execution readiness without hype.

### Aesthetic Direction
Light theme, left-story plus right-checks split, with practical trust language and minimal ornamentation. Avoid cold enterprise dashboard styling.

### Design Principles
1. Show trust through concrete checks, not slogans.
2. Keep copy scannable and decision-oriented for cross-functional buyers.
3. Use balanced layout rhythm with clear left-right responsibilities.
4. Motion stays subtle and supportive only; no spectacle.
5. Prioritize legibility and mobile clarity over decorative density.
